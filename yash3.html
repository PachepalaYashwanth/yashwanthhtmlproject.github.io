CSK have reached Chennai after winning IPL 2023 CSK defeated Hardik Pandya’s
GT by 5 wickets in the final

MS Dhoni’s Chennai Super Kings (CSK) have reached Chennai after winning the 2023 edition of the Indian Premier League (IPL).

On Monday, May 29, Dhoni’s men defeated Hardik Pandya’s Gujarat Titans (GT) by five wickets in the final at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad.
